We started to work on our biggest and most ambitious project so far. Up until now, what we made where 2D games only and most of them where created over a single weekend for the Ludum Dare game development competition. This new project though has a scale of rather months than days, but we will still regulary participate in Ludum Dare to train our game dev skills.

Features:

  • Turn-based Strategy
  • Modern 3D Visuals
  • Story-driven Single-Player Campaign
  • approx. 5 hours of play-time
  • 4 playable factions, each with their unique units
  • Skirmish against an ai or local human opponents
