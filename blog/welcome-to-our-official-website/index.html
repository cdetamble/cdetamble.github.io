Welcome to our official website!

September 16, 2019

Finally, after weeks of hard work in our spare-time, the website's theme it is finally ready to go live. Here you will be informed about our on-going projects and be able to peek a bit behind the scenes. MouthlessGames was founded in 2018, as a successor to The Refactory. Our game development journey started all the way back in August 2016, when we released our first Android app Jewel Thief. Since then, we are regular participants in the Ludum Dare game development competition. Our motivation to do so is not only because it makes a ton of fun, but it is also always incredible how much one learns about one's weaknesses and strengths when it comes to game making.
